Global Online Movement

#newyearofchinese will bring the online Chinese learning community together so we can commit to learning the language more effectively and with the support of others.

Whether you've been studying Chinese for some time or have never spoken a word of Mandarin there will be opportunities to improve together.

We'll be introducing you to the most effective tools for learning different aspects on the Chinese language. Tools and challenges will be matched to vary the area of focus each month, bringing new and exciting learning opportunities throughout the year. Social Competition

Social learning lets you cheer each other on and get props for your own progress. At the same time slacking is harder when others are watching! For good measure we'll also stir in a little bit of friendly competition (with prizes!). Get pep talks, support and useful resources along the way.

Kickoff

In conjunction with the Chinese Newyear and the kickoff of #newyearofmandarin Hacking Chinese and WaiChinese are launching the

Start Today Speaking Challenge- February 1st
This challenge will feature a systematic approach for students of all levels to start or improve their Mandarin speaking. The challenge begins with baseline week where students will practice their speaking using the WaiChinese system. Students will be asked to complete a set of speaking excersises while seeing the shape of their tone. Students submit their recordings and are graded by a real life teacher. Each week there will be now content released through social media and student will progress through content monitored by one of the WaiChinese teachers. All submissions will be graded and progress will be tracked. Each week students will receive progress reports. At the end of the month prizes from our sponsors will be given to the students who improved the most. While we have a larger discussion using #newyearofMandarin
